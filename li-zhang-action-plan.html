Europe's largest acute hospital, located in Edgbaston. Medical staff, visiting consultants, families of long-stay patients and clinical researchers generate consistent 12-month demand — one of the most reliable demand drivers in any UK city.

❓ Your questions answered directly
What happens if the property sells before 6 months?

This came up on the call. If you sell during the fixed term, the agreed completion date is treated as the contract end — you won't be penalised for a sale that was always the plan. The £1,000 early exit fee applies if you simply change your mind, not if the property changes hands. Zac confirmed this directly on the call.

What is the honest worst case if projections are not hit?

Year 1 is a ramp — there will be months where bookings are lighter than the projection. If occupancy came in at 50% instead of 67%, your Year 1 net would be around £16,000 vs £21,938. Still £4,168/year more than long-let. The key point: the property is already furnished, setup cost is minimal, and Harborne has proven year-round demand. The downside is limited.

Can I use the property myself while it's on Airbnb?

Yes — just block dates in the calendar, ideally 2–3 weeks in advance where possible to avoid cancelling confirmed bookings. The property is yours. Stayful works around your personal use — the only ask is as much notice as possible so pricing and availability can be optimised around it.

How does guest damage actually work?

AirCover provides £2.5M damage protection. For smaller claims, Stayful handles everything directly through Airbnb's resolution centre — you never deal with it. Stayful documents the property before and after every stay. The honest answer: cosmetic wear and small breakages happen and aren't worth claiming for; genuine damage is covered and pursued.

What is the 13% management fee conditional on?

Confirming your intent to proceed (signing the management agreement) before 7th July 2026. The property does not need to be Airbnb-ready by then — just a signed agreement. If you sign after the 7th, the standard fee of 15% + VAT applies. The saving on your projected gross is approximately £1,645/year.

How long from signing to going live?

For a fully furnished property like yours, it's faster than most. Key safe installation (1 day), professional photography (half day + 2–3 days editing), listing setup (3–5 days). Realistically 2–3 weeks from signing to live. No furniture sourcing, no staging — just admin and photography.
