❓ Your Questions, Honestly Answered
What happens when the property sells — can I exit the agreement? ▼
Yes. During the initial 6-month term there's an early exit fee of £1,000. After the initial term, either party can terminate with 3 months' written notice. If you sell during an active term and the buyer doesn't want to continue, we work with you on a practical transition — this is a common scenario for SELL-profile owners and we handle it regularly.
Will having guests affect my ability to show the property to buyers? ▼
You manage viewings through our platform calendar. You can block any dates you need for viewings — we just ask for reasonable notice so we can avoid confirmed bookings. Estate agents are experienced with rental properties; in most cases it's a positive signal for buy-to-let buyers.
What is the honest worst case if projections are not hit? ▼
If the market shifts or the property underperforms, the worst realistic case at 80% of our conservative Year 1 projection is approximately £17,500 net — still comfortably above the £11,837 long-let alternative. There is no scenario where a well-presented property in Harborne B32 earns less than a long-let, all other things being equal.
Who handles guests if something goes wrong while I'm dealing with the sale? ▼
Stayful handles 100% of guest communication, issues, and claims. You will not receive calls from guests. Everything routes through Stayful's ops team. If something requires your attention it comes through your Slack channel on a non-urgent basis.
What does AirCover actually cover — is it reliable? ▼
AirCover provides up to £2.5M in host damage protection. It's not perfect — claims under £85 are typically not pursued as the guest retaliation risk on reviews outweighs the value — but for meaningful damage it's a genuine safety net. Stayful handles the entire claims process on your behalf and pre/post-stay photos are taken at every changeover.
Can I use the property personally during the selling period? ▼
Yes — you can block any dates you need for personal use, viewings, or contractor access via the platform calendar. Confirmed guest bookings always take priority, so block dates in advance. We can help you plan a personal-use calendar if needed.
