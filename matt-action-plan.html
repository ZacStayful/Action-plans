What Drives Demand in Brighton
🚂
London Weekend Escapes
Brighton is 50–55 minutes direct from London Victoria and London Bridge, making it the default UK city-break for Londoners. This creates strong, consistent demand every Friday and Saturday night, 52 weeks a year.
🌊
Seafront & Tourism
Brighton's Victorian pier, pebble beach, and SEA LIFE aquarium (0.55km from Pelham Square) attract millions of visitors. Your property is ideally placed for central seafront access — a key search filter on Airbnb.
🎭
Events Calendar
Brighton Fringe (May — Europe's largest arts fringe), Brighton Pride (August — 500,000+ attendees), and a packed year-round events calendar push occupancy to near-100% during key weekends, enabling premium pricing.
🎓
University City
University of Brighton and University of Sussex bring 40,000+ students. Parent visits at enrolment, graduation, and throughout the year provide consistent mid-week and shoulder season demand beyond the typical leisure traveller.
🏥
Medical & Healthcare
The Royal Sussex County Hospital is a major regional medical centre. Patients and relatives travel from across the South East for treatment, generating steady mid-week, off-peak demand that smooths out seasonal troughs.
🎪
The Lanes & North Laine
Pelham Square sits in the heart of North Laine — Brighton's independent shopping and restaurant quarter. Guests search specifically for properties here. The area's identity as a destination in itself commands a rate premium over periphery locations.

Your Questions — Answered Honestly
Will the nightly rates actually be achievable? I saw listings nearby at £78–80/night. ▼
Yes — with the right setup and management. The listings you saw at £78–80 are almost certainly early-stage properties with few reviews, or listings that have never been optimised. Our dynamic pricing model adjusts daily based on local demand signals, events, and competitor movement. £132/night is an annual average across all 365 nights — peak nights in July and August will be £180–200+, and some quieter nights will be lower. The average is what matters, and that's what the comparable data supports.
What if I decide to sell while you're managing the property? ▼
You can absolutely still sell. The process is: give Stayful 3 months' written notice via Slack (rolling term, after the initial 6 months). Any confirmed guest bookings within the notice period would either be honoured or Stayful would help relocate guests. During the sales process, viewings are coordinated around guest stays. The property remains fully managed right up to handover. Nothing about STL prevents a sale — it actually keeps the property in perfect condition throughout.
What is the honest worst case if projections are not hit? ▼
Honest answer: Year 1 will likely come in at 80–90% of full projection — that's why we show the 80% figure as the Year 1 conservative. If performance is significantly below that (say, below £12,000 net in Year 1), we'd have a direct conversation about why — whether that's platform positioning, local supply changes, or property presentation — and address it. The worst realistic case based on Brighton 1-bed data is still likely to exceed long-let net income. But projections are projections, and nothing is guaranteed.
How reliable is Stayful in Brighton? My previous management company pulled out of the market. ▼
This is a completely fair concern and Zac mentioned it on the call. Stayful already has active operations in Brighton — cleaning teams, maintenance contacts, local pricing intelligence. We're not entering the market speculatively. The reason other operators pull out is usually because they're running thin margins with few properties and no local infrastructure. Stayful's model is built for long-term management across multiple cities. We wouldn't be here if Brighton wasn't working.
What's the commitment if I sign? ▼
6-month initial fixed term. If you exit early within those 6 months, there's a £1,000 early exit fee. After the initial term: rolling 3-month written notice — either party can end it. The agreement doesn't prevent you from selling. You don't need to be a full-time landlord; Stayful handles everything day-to-day.
