Your questions, answered honestly

Is the £300 maintenance threshold per repair or for the whole year? ▾
It's per month — Stayful can auto-approve up to £300/month of maintenance work so we can act quickly for guests. Anything above that gets flagged in Slack for your decision first, and it's always called out clearly so there are no surprises on your monthly statement.
How long does onboarding take, and how soon will the first booking come in? ▾
Onboarding typically takes 2–3 weeks. First bookings usually land within 48 hours of the listing going live — the longest we've ever seen is 5 days. Platforms tend to give new listings a visibility boost during this early 'test phase'.
Where do you actually find guests? ▾
We list on Airbnb, Booking.com and our own direct booking site. Around 30% of bookings at comparable properties come through direct relationships with guests who've stayed before — that avoids platform commission and tends to mean longer, lower-maintenance stays.
Does the nearby hospital really help bookings? ▾
Not meaningfully — it's a 20–25 minute drive, which is too far to count as a genuine demand driver, and we've corrected this in your figures. The real strength for 22 Acton Road is the rail links into Long Eaton and entertainment venues like Rock City within a few miles.
What is the honest worst case if these projections aren't hit? ▾
March is consistently the quietest month in this market, and even then our forecast shows around £1,604 net — still ahead of your £1,244 mortgage. Short-let income does fluctuate, and if a month underperforms further than forecast, the margin above your mortgage would be tighter rather than guaranteed. That's exactly why we show Year 1 conservatively at 80% of the full run rate, rather than the optimistic case.
