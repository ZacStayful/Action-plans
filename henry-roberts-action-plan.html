What Drives Demand in Bevington Bush, Liverpool
🏥
NHS & Healthcare
The Royal Liverpool University Hospital — one of the largest NHS teaching hospitals in England — is a short walk from Bevington Bush. Healthcare workers relocating, medical professionals on placements, and patients attending specialist appointments generate consistent year-round demand for short-stay accommodation.
🎓
University City
The University of Liverpool and Liverpool John Moores University are both nearby. Graduation weekends, visiting parents, prospective student open days, and visiting academics create reliable seasonal booking spikes throughout the academic year.
⚽
Football & Major Events
Liverpool FC at Anfield and Everton FC's new Bramley-Moore Dock stadium (opening 2025) both drive strong match-day demand. The M&S Bank Arena and Exhibition Centre regularly host sell-out concerts, conferences, and boxing events drawing visitors from across the UK.
🎸
Tourism & Heritage
Liverpool is a globally recognised tourist destination — The Beatles story, Albert Dock, Tate Liverpool, the waterfront UNESCO World Heritage buffer zone, and a thriving food and nightlife scene. Leisure tourism peaks May–September and again around Christmas markets and New Year.
💼
Business Travel
Liverpool city centre has a strong professional services, legal, and financial sector. Liverpool ONE is the 4th largest retail destination in England. Corporate travellers attending meetings, training, and conferences at the ACC Liverpool convention centre generate mid-week bookings throughout the year.
🔗
Transport Links
Liverpool Lime Street connects to London Euston (2hrs 10min), Manchester (50min), and Birmingham (1hr 40min) — making it accessible for UK short-break travellers. Liverpool John Lennon Airport serves European routes and increases inbound leisure demand particularly in summer.

Your Questions Answered
What notice period do I need to give MyProperty Host?▼
This is something you'll need to check directly in your current agreement — most STL management contracts have a 1–3 month notice period. Once you know your notice date, we can align the Stayful agreement start date accordingly. The good news is that you can sign the Stayful agreement now (locking in the 13%+VAT rate before 31 May) and simply set the commencement date to when your current contract ends. There's no conflict in signing early.
My property is currently at break-even — will Stayful actually make a difference?▼
The issues you described — missed check-ins, damage not reported, account manager turnover, poor reviews — are operational failures that compound over time. Poor reviews suppress visibility; suppressed visibility kills occupancy; low occupancy means your property earns near nothing in winter months. Stayful's model is fundamentally different: we manage far fewer properties per person, which means your property gets actual attention. Even in Year 1, the projection of ~£889/month net is substantially above your current break-even situation.
Can you look at my Burton-on-Trent property too?▼
Absolutely — we discussed this on the call. Once we've reviewed the Liverpool transition plan, we can run a full income analysis on the Burton-on-Trent property. Just send over the address and any details (beds, condition, whether it's tenanted) and we'll have projections back to you within 48 hours.
What's the honest worst case if the projections aren't hit?▼
Year 1 is already built at 80% of the full run-rate — that's the conservative scenario. If Year 1 came in at 70% (below our projection), net income would be around £9,300 for the year — still roughly £775/month, still above what you're currently earning. The comparable Stayful city-centre properties (803 Eastbank Tower in Manchester achieved £35,917 net in 2025) give us confidence in the underlying demand model. The main risk is a slower ramp during onboarding, which is why we always under-promise on Year 1.
How does key access actually work — given the problems I've had?▼
We use a key safe located near the property entrance (we'll identify the best location for yours specifically). The access code is unique per guest and rotated after each stay. We also work with a digital lock / key nest system with full audit trails — so we always know who entered, when. Guest check-in instructions are sent 24 hours before arrival, including video walkthroughs where needed. No more guests waiting outside or missed handovers.
What does the 26% direct booking rate actually mean for me?▼
26% of our portfolio's bookings now come through direct channels (our Stayful booking site and remarketing to past guests) rather than Airbnb or Booking.com. This matters for two reasons: (1) we don't pay the 3% Airbnb host service fee on those bookings, meaning slightly more of the gross comes to you, and (2) it reduces reliance on a single platform — protecting your income if Airbnb ever changes its algorithm or policies.
