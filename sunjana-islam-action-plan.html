🎯 What Drives Demand in Newcastle
🏥
NHS & Hospital Demand
Newcastle Hospitals NHS Trust — Royal Victoria Infirmary and Freeman Hospital — is one of the largest in Europe. Medical staff, visiting specialists and patient families generate consistent year-round demand in NE postcodes.
⚽
Newcastle United FC
St James' Park holds 52,000 fans. Premier League fixtures draw visitors from across the UK and Europe. Match weekends push up occupancy and nightly rates for city-close properties like Heaton Park Road.
🎓
Universities & Visiting Academics
Newcastle and Northumbria universities host 50,000+ students. Graduation weekends, open days, visiting lecturers and parents all drive short-stay demand throughout the academic calendar.
🎉
Stag & Hen Capital
Newcastle is consistently ranked one of the UK's most popular group destinations. Heaton's proximity to the city centre makes it a natural base. Group bookings at higher rates are common and manageable with proper house rules.
🏃
Great North Run
The world's largest half marathon brings 57,000 runners every October. Hotels sell out city-wide. A well-listed Heaton flat commands a significant premium that weekend.
💼
Business & Corporate Travel
Sage, Accenture, NHS procurement and Newcastle City Council generate weekday short-stay demand. Heaton has easy Metro access to the city centre and Quayside business district.

❓ Your Questions — Answered Honestly
QI'm worried about income consistency — what if it's not £24,150 every year?▼
The £24,150 net is based on comparable Newcastle short-lets. Year 1 will likely be lower (~£19,300) as reviews and ranking build. Even at 70% of Year 2, you'd net around £16,900 — still 55% more than your previous £10,800 long-let.
QPass the Keys also cover Newcastle — how does Stayful compare?▼
Pass the Keys is a large national operation; Stayful is selective. We take on fewer properties and give each one more active attention — pricing, listing quality, remarketing. We're confident in our numbers and encourage you to get quotes from both.
QWhat happens if there's damage or a bad guest?▼
AirCover provides up to £2.5M in host damage protection. Most issues are minor and resolved quickly. Stayful coordinates the claim process. For anything Airbnb won't cover, short-let landlord insurance costs around £150–250/year.
QCan I still use the property myself?▼
Absolutely — it's your property. Block dates anytime through the live calendar. We recommend keeping personal use in Oct, Jan, Feb to protect peak income.
QWhat's the honest worst case if projections aren't hit?▼
Honest worst case for a well-prepared Newcastle 2-bed: ~50% occupancy, generating roughly £35,000 gross and £18,000 net — still £7,200/year more than your long-let. The real risk is poor photography or a weak listing — which is why setup quality matters.
