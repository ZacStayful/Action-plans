What drives demand in Kirkham & the Fylde
🏥
Blackpool Victoria Hospital
The NHS trust hospital serves the entire Fylde Coast and is 10 minutes from your property. Medical staff, visiting family members of patients, and NHS contractors book extended stays year-round. This provides reliable mid-week occupancy even in low season.
⭐
Ribby Hall Holiday Village
One of the UK's top 5-star short-break resorts is practically on your doorstep. When Ribby Hall is fully booked — which it regularly is at peak times — guests look for nearby quality accommodation. Your location directly captures this overflow demand.
🎡
Blackpool Tourism
Britain's most visited seaside resort attracts over 17 million visitors annually. Summer bookings, hen and stag parties, the Blackpool Illuminations (September–November), and weekend breaks all generate sustained demand just 5 miles away.
⛳
Lytham St Annes & Golf
Royal Lytham & St Annes Golf Club — home to multiple Open Championships — is 15 minutes away. Golf visitors typically book 3–5 night stays, often midweek, which complements your weekend leisure bookings perfectly and reduces voids.
✈️
BAE Systems & Defence
BAE Systems' major manufacturing and engineering sites at Warton and Samlesbury are 10–15 minutes away, employing thousands. Defence contractors, project visitors, and engineers on rotation need accommodation regularly throughout the year.
🐾
Pet-Friendly Advantage
Your laminate flooring throughout makes this property ideal for a pet-friendly listing — a hugely underserved niche. Pet-friendly properties command a 15–25% premium and experience significantly lower voids. Restricting to ground floor keeps the property protected.

Your questions, answered honestly
Will quiet months earn less than a long-let?
Honestly, yes — some individual months will be lower than a gross long-let rent. March is projected at £1,526 net. But that comparison needs to be fair: a long-let nets ~£1,147/month after agent fees, void allowance, and maintenance. On that basis, even your quietest month (£1,526) is still meaningfully ahead of the long-let net. Annually, STL at ~£29,498 compares to long-let at ~£13,764. The volatility is real and worth weighing — but the annual return is substantially higher.
What's the honest worst case if projections aren't hit?
We project at 56% occupancy based on comparable Fylde properties. If you achieve 40% occupancy instead, you'd net approximately £21,000–22,000/year — still significantly ahead of long-let. We set pricing conservatively in year 1 while the listing builds its review profile. If the property genuinely underperforms vs projections after 6 months, we'll have a frank conversation about whether it makes sense to continue.
What does the furnishing and setup process look like?
We've sent you a detailed setup quote with itemised costs. For a 4-bed unfurnished property, the full setup is approximately £7,350 — covering furniture, bedding, kitchen equipment, photography, and labour. Steven completing the flooring first is the right sequence. Once that's done and you sign the agreement, we can have the property live within 3–4 weeks. We project you'll recover the setup cost within 6 months of going live.
What's the commitment — and what happens if we want to exit?
The agreement has a 6-month initial fixed term. If you want to exit during that window, there's a £1,000 early exit fee. After 6 months, it rolls onto a 3-month notice period — either party can exit with 3 months' written notice, no penalty. The 6-month initial term protects both parties: it gives the listing time to build properly and ensures we recoup our onboarding investment. Most owners are seeing strong results well within 6 months.
How does guest damage get handled?
Airbnb's AirCover provides £2.5M damage protection per stay. We photograph the property before and after every guest stay as evidence. If damage occurs, we raise the claim with Airbnb on your behalf — you never deal with them directly. For issues below £300 (the vast majority), we authorise repairs immediately without calling you. Above £300, we'll always get your sign-off before proceeding.
Steven does maintenance — how does that work with Stayful?
That works really well. We'll add Steven to your Slack channel so he can see when maintenance issues come up and respond directly. For urgent issues up to £300, we'll flag it on Slack and he can decide if he wants to handle it himself or if we should arrange it. It keeps costs down and means you have a trusted person who knows the property looking after it. We just need 72 hours' notice before any property access to ensure there are no guest conflicts.
