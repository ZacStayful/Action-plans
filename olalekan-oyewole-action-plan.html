🚂
Turkey Street Station — 3 Minutes Away

🏥
Barnet Hospital — Healthcare Visitor Demand
Barnet Hospital generates consistent off-peak bookings from families of patients, medical professionals on rotation, and healthcare staff from across North London. These are typically mid-week, multi-night stays — exactly the segment that drives occupancy in your slower months.
🌿
Lee Valley Regional Park — Leisure & Events
The Lee Valley corridor brings leisure visitors year-round — cycling, watersports, walking trails, and the White Water Centre. Event-goers and activity weekenders provide a secondary demand layer on top of the corporate and healthcare base.

Contractor & Business Accommodation

❓ Your Questions — Answered Honestly
What is the honest worst case if projections are not hit?+
Year 1 is already shown at 80% of the projection — that's the built-in downside buffer. If performance is below that (say 70%), your worst-case annual net would be around £13,688, which is still broadly level with what a long-let would return. The difference: you retain full flexibility with short-let. There's no guarantee on bookings, but the floor in this location is genuinely supported by hospital, transport and contractor demand.
How does the ramp-up period actually work?+
Month 1-3: Airbnb's algorithm needs booking history before it will surface your listing prominently. Expect occupancy of 40-60% initially. Month 4-8: As reviews build and pricing is refined, occupancy and rates increase. Months 9-12: By this point you're in the data and the algorithm actively promotes the listing. The 80% Year 1 figure accounts for this — it's the expected real-world trajectory, not a worst case.
What happens if there's a difficult guest or damage?+
Every booking on Airbnb comes with AirCover — up to £2.5M damage protection, theft cover, and liability insurance. Stayful manages the claim from start to finish: pre-stay documentation, post-stay inspection, and lodging the claim directly with Airbnb. You don't deal with guests or Airbnb at all. For repairs under £300 we authorise and sort it without calling you.
Can I still access and use my property?+
Yes — your booking calendar is visible in real time. You can block dates for personal use at any time. The only practical requirement is 72-hour advance notice via the Slack channel so any confirmed bookings can be protected. Stayful will never accept a booking during your blocked dates. The property is yours.
What are the real ongoing costs I need to know about?+
Your owner costs: gas (~£42/month), WiFi (£35/month), council tax (£175/month) = ~£252/month total. On top, Stayful's management fee (15%+VAT) and the £42/month software fee are deducted from your gross before payout — so you receive the net figure. The only one-time setup items are professional photography (~£250) and key safes (~£30). Maintenance over £300 is always signed off by you first.
What happens if I change my mind after signing?+
During the initial 6-month term there's a £1,000 early exit fee to cover onboarding costs. After the 6-month term is up, you're on a 3-month rolling notice — no fee, no penalty. That's a maximum 9 months of commitment from signing. Most owners who are uncertain choose to sign for the full term and reassess at month 6 — by that point the data makes the decision obvious.
