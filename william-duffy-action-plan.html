What Drives Demand in Brighton

🌊
UK's Premier Coastal Destination
Brighton & Hove attract over 11 million visitors annually. The seafront, Royal Pavilion, and the Lanes draw leisure visitors from across the UK with consistent year-round demand.
🚂
London Gateway — 55 Minutes Away
Just 55–60 minutes from London Victoria by train. Brighton is the weekend escape destination for millions of Londoners, generating premium short-break demand and elevated Friday–Sunday rates.
🎉
Major Events Calendar
Brighton Pride (500,000+ attendees in August), Brighton Festival (May), The Great Escape music festival, and Brighton Centre conferences drive occupancy spikes and well-above-average nightly rates throughout the year.
🎓
University City
University of Brighton and University of Sussex enrol 30,000+ students, creating steady demand from visiting parents, open day guests, graduations, and academic visitors year-round.
🏢
Conference & Business Market
Brighton Centre hosts major political party conferences, national trade shows, and corporate events. These bring high-spend professional visitors requiring multiple-night stays with flexible dates.
🌞
Year-Round Appeal
Unlike many coastal destinations, Brighton performs strongly in winter thanks to its thriving arts, food, and nightlife scene — even February nets £2,056 on this property, well above the long-let equivalent.

Your Questions — Answered Honestly

We can get you set up in the system now and start the onboarding paperwork, but our management responsibilities don't begin until the property is in a lettable condition. Once the renovation wraps up, onboarding typically takes 7–14 working days — we complete the listing setup, arrange photography, and connect the property to all platforms. You'd be live within a couple of weeks of finishing the works.

A 6-bedroom unfurnished property needs everything — beds, soft furnishings, kitchen kit, electrical items, photography, and the minor touches that get it guest-ready. Your setup quote (SQ-2026-071) gives you the full itemised breakdown. Total is in the region of £9,920. Payment is 100% upfront. We can coordinate the entire setup — you hand over the keys and we handle it. You don't need to be there.

The Year 1 figures (£29,114 net) already account for a new listing ramp-up — they're 80% of the full run-rate. In Brighton, new listings typically gain review traction within 6–8 weeks when priced correctly and staged well. The Year 1 figures are genuinely achievable for a well-presented 6-bed in this area. The Year 2+ figure (£36,393) represents an established listing performing at full potential.

The worst realistic case — shown in the presentation — is around £2,056/month net (February performance every month). That's still well above the long-let benchmark of £1,166/month, so even in the most pessimistic scenario you're ahead. If the listing genuinely underperforms for sustained periods, we'd have a frank conversation and re-evaluate the strategy — including pricing, photography, or positioning. We earn through your performance, so underperformance is our problem too.

Every Airbnb stay comes with AirCover — £2.5M damage protection. We take a security deposit from all guests. Pre and post-stay photography documents the condition. If damage occurs, Stayful manages the entire claim process — you never deal with Airbnb or the guest directly. For damage not covered by AirCover (rare), we pursue the guest via the security deposit. Minor maintenance up to £300 we handle without bothering you.

After the initial 6-month fixed term, either party can exit with 3 months' written notice. If you exit during the initial 6-month term early, there's a £1,000 early exit fee — this covers the onboarding investment we make. All confirmed guest bookings at the time of notice are always honoured. There's no long-term lock-in beyond the initial period.
