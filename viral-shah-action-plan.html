⚽
Bramall Lane — Sheffield United FC
Two minutes from the stadium, Shoreham Street sits in one of the most bookable locations in S2. Match days, cup games, and stadium events generate a reliable surge in demand that standard long-lets miss entirely. Sheffield United's Championship profile means a full fixture calendar.
🏙️
City Centre Proximity
S2 is a 10–15 minute walk from Sheffield's city centre, the Devonshire Quarter, and the O2 Academy — making it equally attractive to weekend leisure guests, corporate travellers, and visitors attending concerts and events at Sheffield Arena.
🎓
University Visitors & Graduations
With two major universities — Sheffield and Sheffield Hallam — the city generates year-round demand from visiting academics, parents' weekends, and graduation weeks, particularly in July and autumn term start.
🏔️
Peak District Gateway
Sheffield borders the Peak District National Park directly. Walkers, cyclists, and outdoor enthusiasts frequently use Sheffield as a base — particularly in spring and summer — giving a natural boost to shoulder and peak season occupancy.
🏭
Kelham Island & Creative Quarter Growth

✈️
Northern Business Travel
Sheffield's manufacturing revival and growing digital sector attract consistent midweek corporate demand, which helps maintain occupancy and strong ADR in lower leisure months.

❓ Your Questions — Answered Honestly

Is the STL income really achievable for a 2-bed in S2?▼
Yes — and the figures are based on comparable properties already operating in Sheffield S2, not theoretical best-case scenarios. The caveat is the Year 1 ramp: new listings take 6–9 months to build reviews, and we project Year 1 at 80% of run rate specifically because of this. Once you have 30–40 reviews and a strong rating, the figures are realistic and often exceeded.
What's the honest worst case if projections are not hit?▼
If the property significantly underperforms projections, you can give 3 months' written notice after the initial 6-month term and return it to long-let. In Year 1, the conservative projection still nets you £14,888 — more than the £9,024 long-let equivalent. The downside floor is higher than the LTL ceiling.
How does the student-let to short-let switch work?▼
Once your tenancy ends, the switch is straightforward: you notify us, we arrange onboarding (typically 7–14 working days), photograph the property, list it, and you're live. If the student arrangement falls through and you want to switch sooner, we can plan the conversion together — just give us 4–6 weeks notice.
How does business rates work — and when does council tax stop?▼
You continue paying council tax during the transition period. Once the property is registered as a short-term let via the VOA, the council tax is refunded (typically after about a year) and you move to business rates. Your first property is likely zero business rates under small business relief. Subsequent properties are assessed on rateable value — typically about half of rateable value as your annual bill.
What happens if a guest damages something?▼
AirCover provides up to £2.5M in guest damage protection. Stayful manages all claims on your behalf — you never deal with Airbnb directly. For smaller items under £300, we have authority to arrange repair without prior approval. Our pre and post-stay photography means there's always a clear record.
What's the rating ramp-up and how long does it take?▼
It takes approximately 30–40 reviews to reach above-average rating, which we expect within 6–9 months. During that period you can expect 5–10% below the projection figures. Once you're above average, you can expect 5–10% above — which is how we achieve the Year 2 run rate figures.
⏰ Why Timing Matters
Summer is the highest-earning period for Sheffield S2 — July alone generates £1,921 net at full run rate. Every month of delay in going live is a month of peak-season income not earned. If you want to be live for the next summer peak, the agreement would need to be signed no later than early autumn 2026.
